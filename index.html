About Me

Welcome! My name is Alyssa J Pupping. I have been an educator for over ten years and recently began the transition to a career in tech. My skills in creating lesson plans have been very useful in website design. I began dabbling in programming when I was just twelve years old, but fell out of practice when I went to college. A lot has changed since then, and I am excited to continue this journey! On a personal note, I recently moved back home to California after living in Oregon for several years. I also spent a year living in New York City, and a summer as a nanny in England. I have two cats (Jasmine and Jupiter) and one dog (Bagby). I enjoy baking, painting, and solving puzzles.
